## Thesis
Despite a ~10% YTD decline and broader software sector weakness, Palantir's underlying fundamentals show sharp re-acceleration driven by AIP adoption in US commercial markets. The combination of hypergrowth and expanding profitability—rare among SaaS peers—keeps the long-term bull case intact, though the premium valuation remains a persistent debate point.

## Key numbers
- Revenue growth: accelerated to 70% YoY in Q4, up 57 points over 10 quarters; Q1 guidance at 73.6%
- US commercial revenue: +137% YoY, +29% QoQ to $507M; annualized run rate doubled to $2B in 2025
- International commercial: only +8% YoY to $170M—a notable weak spot
- Adjusted operating margin: record 57.4%; Q1 guided at ~56.8%
- Rule of 40: 127%, up 46 pts YoY and 13 pts QoQ
- Adjusted FCF: $791M in Q4 (56% margin); 2026 FCF guided up ~77% YoY to ~$3.9–4.1B
- NRR: 139%, up 5 pts QoQ and 19 pts YoY
- RPO: $4.21B, +143% YoY, +62% QoQ
- Record TCV: $4.26B, +138% YoY; commercial TCV $2.6B, +161% YoY
- Government revenue: +60% YoY to $730M (~52% of total revenue)
- FY26 guidance: ~61% revenue growth; US commercial >115% YoY
- Forward P/S: ~49.85x vs. well below historical medians

## Decision-relevant takeaways
- AIP's ontology-based architecture handles fragmented/incomplete data—a structural edge over traditional BI and generic LLM competitors.
- The 2025 beat/raise pattern (initial FY25 guide of ~31% grew to 56% actual) suggests FY26's 61% guide could prove conservative.
- EV/EBITDA has compressed to levels last seen during the April 2025 selloff, making the valuation more attractive on earnings-based metrics than the headline P/S implies.
- Government pipeline strengthening: $10B Army contract, $448M Navy deal, and Pentagon signaling larger/longer AI contracts.

## Risks / watch-fors
- Anthropic's "supply chain risk" designation by the Pentagon could complicate Maven Smart System deployments; model-agnostic alternatives exist but add execution uncertainty.
- International commercial growth at only 8% YoY is a meaningful drag and diverges sharply from US momentum.
- Technical downside scenario: if price fails to hold above ~$186–$195, Elliott Wave analysis targets a correction to $87–$119 support zone; bull thesis invalidated below $66.
- Valuation at ~50x forward sales leaves little margin for any execution miss.
